About D&D Character Manager

Welcome to D&D Character Manager, your ultimate tool for creating and managing your Dungeons & Dragons characters. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or just starting your journey, our platform makes it easy to create, track, and develop your characters.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an intuitive and powerful platform for tabletop RPG players to manage their characters. We believe that managing your character sheet should be as fun as playing the game itself.

Features

Character Creation

Easily create detailed character sheets with all the essential information about your D&D characters.

Character Management

Update and manage your characters as they grow and develop throughout your campaigns.

Visual Customization

Upload character images and customize their appearance to bring your characters to life.

Backstory & Personality

Keep track of your character's backstory, personality traits, and development over time.

Progressive Web Application

This project will be developed as a Progressive Web Application (PWA), enabling offline capabilities and an app-like experience. The Flask backend will handle the server-side logic, and client-side interactions will be managed using JavaScript.

Project Goals

This project teaches students the integration of various web development technologies, including:

  • Building multi-page web applications using Flask
  • Creating a responsive UI with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Handling CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete) with JSON-based data
  • Documenting functionality using flowcharts and diagrams
  • Deploying the application on a live server (e.g., PythonAnywhere)
